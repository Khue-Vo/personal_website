About Me

Hello! I am a Computer Science senior with a minor in Business Administration at the University of St. Thomas. My journey in technology is driven by a fascination with systems-thinking, symbolic logic, and the mechanics of how things connect—whether that is code, data, or people.

As a developer, my engineering background is rooted in full-stack architecture, building secure relational databases, and designing robust backend APIs. At the same time, my work as a CRM and digital marketing intern has allowed me to sharpen my strategic communication and cross-functional agility in fast-paced environments.

I thrive at the intersection of technical execution and human-centered problem solving. Rather than just writing clean, scalable code, I focus on understanding the business logic behind it, translating complex backend systems into real-world value, and collaborating across multidisciplinary teams to build meaningful digital experiences.
